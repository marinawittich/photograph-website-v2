"Where passion meets professionalism."

Hello, welcome to my website! I’m Alena, you can call me Lena. I’m a Russian speaking photographer based in Aurora, CO.

My family and I used to live in a small village with a garden and farm animals so I was always inspired by nature. From an early childhood I was a creative soul drawn to a different kinds of art. My photography journey started in March of 2021 and I’m absolutely love doing it. I use cozy dark tones in my photography and editing. Photoshop retouching is included in every session as well.

My goal is to give you a very pleasant experience from the moment you reach out to me all the way until delivery of your gallery. I will help you to choose outfits for your session, hair and makeup touch ups can also be included if needed.

I cannot wait for you to reach out to me, so we can start planning your next session!

MY MISSION

My mission is to capture the beauty and essence of life's most precious moments through stunning and timeless photographs.

I believe that every moment, no matter how big or small, deserves to be celebrated and preserved.

My passion for photography drives us to create exceptional images that tell the story of your life, whether it's a wedding, a family gathering, or a personal project.
